ABOUT US

Arutu is an African Gaming Initiative that seeks to improve the African videogaming experience. Our vision is to be the epicenter of the African Gaming Industry. This simply means we want to be front and center of all African Gaming Activities. People should look to us whenever they want the best support possible for their gaming ideas and events. We are achieveing this through game production, community building and education programmes. Fun Fact; We are the first fully African Team to be awarded the DIVERSE GAME DEVELOPERS GRANT FUND by the International Game Developers Association. The fund was awarded for the prototype development of our innovative hypercasual game; CONQUEROR OF WORDS.
